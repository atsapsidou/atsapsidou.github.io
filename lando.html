Lando Norris

Lando Norris is a British-Belgian Formula 1 driver known for his speed, charisma, and strong presence both on and off the track. Born on November 13, 1999, in Bristol, England, he races for McLaren F1 Team and is considered one of the most promising talents of his generation. He began karting at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks of junior motorsport, winning several prestigious titles before making his Formula 1 debut in 2019 with McLaren. Norris is admired for his racecraft, consistency, and fan-friendly personality, often engaging with supporters via social media and streaming platforms.
